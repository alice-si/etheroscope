Etheroscope

Browse Smart Contracts the Smart Way.

Etheroscope allows you to look up or search for your favourite Ethereum smart contracts and inspect their changes over time. Our technology makes use of cutting-edge algorithms to rapidly scan over 4 million blocks in the Ethereum blockchain. We deliver the data that matters to you, to you, all in real-time. Start exploring the blockchain with us today!

Etheroscope is developed in co-operation with alice.si, a social-impact startup.
